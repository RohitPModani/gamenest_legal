# How to Delete Your Data in Game Nest

**Last updated: November 29, 2025**

This guide explains how you can manage and delete your data stored in the Game Nest app. All data is stored locally on your device, and you have full control over what to keep or remove.

## üìã What Data is Stored

Game Nest stores the following information locally on your device:

### Game Data
- **High scores** and best times for all 25 games
- **Statistics** (wins, losses, draws) for AI-based games
- **Game progress** and completed puzzles
- **Difficulty-specific records** (easy, medium, hard levels)

### App Preferences
- **Favorite games list** (your personalized favorites category)
- **Theme preference** (Dark Neon, Light, Pixie, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Cyberpunk, Midnight, Aurora, Desert, or Space)
- **App settings** and UI preferences
- **Timer and stopwatch** session history
- **Pomodoro focus** session statistics

**Important**: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers.

### Method 1: Reset All Statistics (Recommended)

This method clears all game statistics and high scores while keeping your app preferences (theme, favorites, etc.).

**Steps:**
1. Open the Game Nest app
2. Tap the **Settings** icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the top-right corner of the hub screen
3. Scroll down to the **"Data"** section
4. Tap **"Reset Statistics"**
5. Read the warning message carefully
6. Tap **"Reset"** to confirm

**What this clears:**
- ‚úÖ All game high scores
- ‚úÖ All win/loss/draw statistics
- ‚úÖ Best times and accuracy records
- ‚úÖ Game progress and completion data

**What this keeps:**
- ‚úÖ Your favorite games list
- ‚úÖ Your theme preference
- ‚úÖ App settings

### Method 2: Clear Individual Game Statistics

Some games have built-in options to reset their specific statistics without affecting other games.

#### Board Games (AI Statistics)
For games like **Tic Tac Toe**, **Connect 4**, **Checkers**, **Reversi**, **Mancala**, **Dots & Boxes**, and **Ultimate Tic Tac Toe**:

1. Open the game from the hub screen
2. Look for a **"Statistics"** or **"Reset Stats"** button on the home screen
3. Tap the reset option
4. Confirm the action

This will clear only that game's win/loss/draw records.

#### Other Games
Most games don't have individual reset buttons. To clear their data, use Method 1 (Reset All Statistics) or Method 4 (Clear All App Data).

---

### Method 3: Clear Favorite Games

To remove games from your favorites list:

1. Navigate to the game you want to remove
2. Look for the **star icon** (‚≠ê) or favorite button
3. Tap it to un-favorite the game

Alternatively:
- Go to the **Favourites** category in the hub
- Open a favorite game
- Remove it from favorites using the star icon

**Note**: This only removes games from your favorites list. It does not delete game statistics or progress.

---

### Method 4: Clear All App Data (Via Device Settings)

This method completely removes all app data, including preferences, favorites, and statistics. The app will be reset to its initial state.

#### On Android:

1. Open your device's **Settings** app
2. Go to **Apps** (or **Applications**)
3. Find and tap **Game Nest**
4. Tap **Storage** (or **Storage & cache**)
5. Tap **Clear Storage** (or **Clear Data**)
6. Confirm the action

Alternatively:
1. Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Game Nest
2. Tap the three-dot menu (‚ãÆ)
3. Select **Uninstall** (then reinstall) or use **Clear Data** if available

#### On iOS:

1. Open your device's **Settings** app
2. Scroll down and tap **Game Nest**
3. Tap **Delete App**
4. Confirm by tapping **Delete**
5. Reinstall from the App Store if desired

**Important**: This will permanently delete:
- ‚úÖ All game statistics and high scores
- ‚úÖ Favorite games list
- ‚úÖ Theme preferences
- ‚úÖ All app settings

---

### Method 5: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.

#### On Android:
1. Long-press the Game Nest app icon
2. Tap **Uninstall** (or drag to Uninstall)
3. Confirm the action

#### On iOS:
1. Long-press the Game Nest app icon
2. Tap the **X** or **Remove App**
3. Confirm by tapping **Delete App**

**Note**: After uninstalling, all app data is permanently deleted. If you reinstall, you'll start fresh with default settings.

## üì± Third-Party Data (Advertising)

Game Nest uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect some information for personalized ads:

- **Advertising ID** (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Approximate location (based on IP address)
- Ad interaction data

### How to Control Advertising Data:

#### Android:
1. Settings ‚Üí Google ‚Üí Ads
2. Tap **"Opt out of Ads Personalization"**
3. Optional: Tap **"Delete advertising ID"**

#### iOS:
1. Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Apple Advertising
2. Turn off **"Personalized Ads"**
3. Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Tracking
4. Turn off **"Allow Apps to Request to Track"**

**Note**: Disabling personalized ads will not stop ads from showing, but they will be less relevant to your interests.

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I recover deleted data?
**A**: No. Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All data is stored locally on your device, so there are no backups on our servers.

### Q: Will deleting data remove ads?
**A**: No. The app will continue to show advertisements. Data deletion only affects your game statistics and preferences.

### Q: Do I need an internet connection to delete data?
**A**: No. All data deletion can be done offline since data is stored locally on your device.

### Q: Can I export my data before deleting?
**A**: Currently, there is no built-in export feature. All data is stored in a format specific to the app.

### Q: Will uninstalling and reinstalling remove ads?
**A**: No. Ads are part of the app's functionality and will continue to appear after reinstalling.

### Q: What happens to my data if I update the app?
**A**: App updates typically preserve your local data. However, we recommend backing up important information by taking screenshots of high scores if needed.
