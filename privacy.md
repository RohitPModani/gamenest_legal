**Last updated: November 29, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Game Nest** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Personal Data
We **do not** require you to create an account to use Game Nest. We **do not** collect, store, or transmit your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information (PII) to our servers.

### 1.2. Local Data Storage
The App stores certain data locally on your device using Flutter's "Shared Preferences" system. This includes:

#### Game Data
- Game high scores, statistics, and progress for all 25 games
- Win/loss/draw records for AI-based games (Tic Tac Toe, 4 in a Row, Checkers, Reversi, Mancala, Dots & Boxes)
- Game preferences and difficulty settings
- Completed puzzle solutions (Sudoku, Nonogram, etc.)

#### App Preferences
- List of favorite games in your personalized favorites category
- Selected theme preference (Dark Neon, Light, Pixie, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Cyberpunk, Midnight, Aurora, Desert, or Space)
- App settings and UI preferences
- Timer and stopwatch session history
- Pomodoro focus session statistics

**Important**: This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, this information will be permanently lost.

### 1.3. Third-Party Data Collection (Advertising)
Game Nest uses **Google AdMob** to display banner advertisements that allow us to provide the app for free. AdMob may collect and use certain information to provide personalized ads, including:

- **Device Identifiers**: Advertising ID (AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise location)
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with ads and app usage patterns
- **Ad Performance Data**: Click-through rates and ad engagement metrics

For detailed information on Google AdMob's data practices, please refer to:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google's Partner Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)
- [AdMob Help Center](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal data directly, our use of information is limited to:

### 2.1. App Functionality
- **Game Progress**: Using local storage to save your scores, achievements, and game states
- **Personalization**: Maintaining your favorites list and app preferences
- **Performance**: Ensuring smooth gameplay and optimal user experience

### 2.2. Monetization
- **Ad Display**: Using AdMob to display relevant advertisements
- **App Sustainability**: Revenue from ads allows us to maintain and improve the app

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1. What We Don't Share
We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties because we don't collect personal information in the first place.

### 3.2. Third-Party Services
- **Google AdMob**: As detailed in section 1.3, AdMob may collect certain data for advertising purposes
- **App Stores**: Download and usage statistics may be provided to Google Play Store and Apple App Store as part of their standard analytics

### 3.3. Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Your Rights and Choices

### 4.1. Advertising Preferences
You can control personalized advertising through your device settings:

**Android:**
- Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID

**iOS:**
- Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off)
- Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (turn off)

### 4.2. Data Management
- **Local Data**: You can clear all locally stored game data through your device's app settings
- **Complete Removal**: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device
- **Reset Options**: Many games include built-in options to reset statistics and progress

### 4.3. App Permissions
Game Nest only requests permissions necessary for functionality:
- **Internet**: Required only for displaying ads (the app works offline otherwise)
- **No location, camera, or contact access** required

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Game Nest is designed as a family-friendly application suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

### 5.1. Parental Controls
- Parents can disable personalized ads through device settings
- The app does not contain social features or chat functionality
- All games are single-player or local multiplayer (no online interaction)

### 5.2. Contact for Parents
If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at voythrix@gmail.com so we can address the situation.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1. Local Data Protection
- All game data is stored locally using Flutter's secure storage mechanisms
- No sensitive data is transmitted over networks
- App uses standard platform security features

### 6.2. Ad Network Security
- We rely on Google AdMob's security measures for ad-related data
- All ad communications use encrypted connections (HTTPS)

## 7. International Data Transfers

Since Game Nest operates primarily with local data storage, international data transfers are limited to:
- AdMob's advertising network (governed by Google's privacy policies)
- App store analytics (Google Play Store, Apple App Store)

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1. Local Data
- Game progress and statistics are retained locally until you delete them or uninstall the app
- No automatic deletion or expiration of locally stored data

### 8.2. Ad Network Data
- AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies
- You can request deletion of ad data through Google's privacy tools

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:
- Changes in app features or functionality
- Updates to third-party service policies
- Legal or regulatory requirements

### 9.1. Notification of Changes
- Significant changes will be communicated through app updates
- The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates the most recent revision
- Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy
